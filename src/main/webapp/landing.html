Who We Are

Right now, there is a great opportunity to re-imagine education, from pre-school through lifelong learning and across the student experience. In the next decade, we will see an incredible influx of new ideas that challenge the status quo and support learners in new ways. As we continue to write our story, we’re working with you to solve some of the biggest problems in education. As a result, we’ve built the best technologies and solutions to help advance our industry in meaningful ways.

But we’re not building new learning products; we’re building a new way for learning to happen. Our goal is to make learning more desirable, accessible, and meaningful for students. By doing this, we have a shared sense of purpose with teachers and administrators at all levels that are working to improve outcomes for learners.

Benefits

Students love it

Student engagement is at the heart of iLearn. Give learners accessibility through mobile devices, assignments at their fingertips through easy navigation, keep them connected to the academic community.

Create time through process effectiveness

Save educators’ time. Basic teaching processes flow more smoothly with iLearn's tools, such as easy to create grades and assessment.

Become more agile

To be competitive an Institution needs to be light on its feet. There's chart and graph representations of exam marks, mobile capabilities to make learning easier, and tools to create more effective assessment.
